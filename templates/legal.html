NeuralMail is an AI-powered email generation tool. We only collect what is necessary to operate the service. We do not sell your data — ever.

01 / OVERVIEW

What Is This Policy?

This Privacy Policy explains how NeuralMail ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects the information you provide when you use our platform. By creating an account or using our services, you agree to the practices described in this document.

We are committed to maintaining your trust and keeping your personal information secure.

02 / DATA COLLECTION

Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Account Data: Your full name, company name, and email address provided at signup.
  • Authentication Data: Your password, stored as a secure cryptographic hash (bcrypt). We never store plaintext passwords.
  • Usage Data: Campaign logs, generated email content, target URLs, and timestamps — linked to your account to provide history and analytics.
  • SMTP Credentials (Optional): If you provide your email SMTP details for sending, these are stored encrypted and used only to dispatch emails on your behalf.

03 / DATA USAGE

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to deliver and improve the NeuralMail service:

  • To authenticate your identity and maintain your session
  • To generate AI-powered email drafts using your provided context (website URL)
  • To display your campaign history, statistics, and analytics dashboard
  • To allow you to send emails via your configured SMTP credentials
  • To improve platform performance and detect technical issues

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties.

04 / THIRD PARTIES

External Services

NeuralMail integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini AI API: Your target website URL is sent to Gemini to generate email content. No personal account data is shared. Please review Google's AI Terms.
  • Gmail (Optional): When you use "Open in Gmail", you are redirected to Gmail's own interface in a new tab. NeuralMail does not send or access your Gmail account directly.

We do not use any advertising networks, analytics trackers, or data brokers.

05 / DATA STORAGE

Where Your Data Lives

All data is stored locally in a SQLite database on the server running NeuralMail. Your data is not replicated to external cloud databases by default.

We retain campaign logs for up to 20 most recent entries per user. You may delete any log entry at any time from your dashboard. Account data is retained for as long as your account is active.

06 / YOUR RIGHTS

Control Over Your Data

You have the following rights at any time:

  • Access the data associated with your account
  • Edit your profile name and role from the dashboard
  • Delete individual campaign log entries
  • Request complete account deletion by contacting us
